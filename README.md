# Quantium Retail Analytics: From Customer Insight to Validated Growth Strategy

An end-to-end retail category analytics engagement — customer segmentation, causal trial evaluation, and executive reporting — completed as part of the Quantium Retail Strategy & Analytics Virtual Experience Program.

📄 **[Read the full case study →](docs/CASE_STUDY.md)**

## Overview

A supermarket chain's Category Manager needed a data-driven foundation for the chip category's next strategic plan: which customers matter most, and whether a trialed store layout change should be rolled out chain-wide. This project analyzed a full year of loyalty transaction data (272 stores) and a three-store layout trial, then translated the findings into a client-ready executive report.

## Key Findings

- **Older and Young Families on Budget plans** are the highest-value customer segments — driven by purchase *frequency*, not basket size
- Chip pricing is essentially **flat across Budget, Mainstream, and Premium tiers** — "Premium" doesn't mean higher per-unit spend in this category
- **Kettle leads by revenue; Doritos shows genuine pricing power** despite lower volume
- The store layout trial produced a **statistically significant lift in one store** (p = 0.040), a borderline trend in a second (p = 0.054), and no effect in a third (p = 0.127) — evidence for an expanded second-phase trial, not an immediate chain-wide rollout
